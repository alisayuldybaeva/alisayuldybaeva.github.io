About Alisa Yuldybaeva

A self-proclaimed modern Reinassance woman, constantly collecting new skills with the belief that anything can be achieved if you set your mind to it.

Born in a small Russian city called Ufa, into a family of musicians, artists, veterinarians, engineers, and artisans, Alisa was always a hyperactive and curious child, exploring, learning about the world, and never staying still.

After moving to Bangkok with her parents, Alisa attended an international school based on the British system, receiving influence from various cultures as she was growing up. Excelling in arts, calligraphy, and computers, she decided to pursue her education in multimedia with a focus on technology at the Bangkok University International College.

Since, Alisa has worked in cinema, advertising, education, and journalism taking on roles as art director, photographer, retoucher, visual designer, teacher, and make-up artist.

In her free time, she loves to draw, cook, handcraft, read, play games, meet people, practice yoga, and explore the outdoors.
